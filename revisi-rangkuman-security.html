A Modern Approach to Digital Security

Your Digital Smartphone

Presented by: Ahmad Muyassar Yani

Your Google Account is Your Smartphone

It holds your most important "apps" and "contacts".

On this "smartphone," you have:

  • "Contacts" (Student Data & Emails)
  • "Photo Gallery" (Lesson Plans & Materials)
  • "Messages" (Gmail)

Protecting this "phone" is essential. Let's learn how.

Feature #1: The Lock Screen

Your password is your screen lock. A long phrase is harder to "guess" than a complex pattern.

Which lock screen is more secure? Click to choose.

Feature #2: Login Notifications

2-Step Verification is like a notification on your phone asking, "Is this you trying to log in?"

Are you trying to sign in?

A device in Jakarta, Indonesia is trying to sign in to your account.

Also, use the **Security Checkup** like checking your phone's "App Permissions" to see who has access.

Feature #3: Scam Message Blocker

Phishing is like a scam SMS. Can you spot the scam below?

Principal Office

Hi team, just a reminder about our staff meeting tomorrow at 2 PM. See you there!

0812-XXXX-XXXX

Plgn Yth, akun Google Anda bermasalah. Segera verifikasi data Anda di bit.ly/xxxxxxxx utk menghindari blokir.

Feature #4: Smart Sharing

Sharing files is like sending a photo on WhatsApp. Choose wisely:

"Send to One Person" (Viewer)

Share information with parents. They can see it, but not change it.

"Share in a Group Chat" (Editor)

Collaborate with colleagues. Everyone in the group can edit.

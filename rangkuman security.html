School Data Security

A Structured & Interactive Guide

Presented by: Group 1 PIGP

Why Teacher Accounts Are Critical

Your account is a gateway to sensitive student data.

It contains:

  • Student Grades
  • Full Names & Emails
  • Personal Information & More

Protecting your account = Protecting our students.

Principle 1: Strong Passwords

Length beats complexity. Click the stronger password:

  • Use unique passwords for every account.
  • Utilize the Google Password Manager.

Principle 2: Safeguard Your Account

Use Google's powerful, built-in tools.

Action 1: Turn On 2-Step Verification

Activate your account's "second lock".

  1. Go to myaccount.google.com
  2. Click the "Security" tab.
  3. Turn on "2-Step Verification".

Action 2: Run a Security Checkup

Google's guided tour to strengthen your account.

  • Review logged-in devices.
  • Manage third-party app access.
  • Get personalized security tips.

Principle 3: Spot Phishing Scams

Click the card to reveal the correct action.

You get an email from "Google Support"...

"Your account is compromised! Click here immediately to verify your password and secure your account."

Action: It's a Scam!

  • NEVER click the link.
  • REPORT it as Phishing in Gmail.
  • Trust the warning banners Google provides.

Principle 4: Smart Daily Habits

Secure your files and your physical environment.

Sharing on Drive

Give the right permissions:

  • Viewer: To share information.
  • Editor: For active collaboration.

Device & Browser Security

✔ Lock your screen when you step away.
✔ Be cautious on public Wi-Fi.
